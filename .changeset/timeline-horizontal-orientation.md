`Timeline` gains an `orientation` prop. The default stays `'vertical'` (the stacked activity feed); `orientation="horizontal"` lays the items left-to-right along a horizontal rail — a step/progress reading for a few stages where width is plentiful (e.g. an approval flow across the top of a page), with the marker above each step and the title/timestamp beneath. Additive and non-breaking.
